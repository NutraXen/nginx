Create your Nginx Server configs here.
- 1 Config per site.

After creating the configuration create a symbolic link to nginx/etc/sites-available & nginx/etc/sites-enabaled
Sample:

ln -s  /usr/share/nginx/vhosts/www-mysite-com.conf /etc/nginx/sites-available/www-mysite-com.conf

ln -s  /usr/share/nginx/vhosts/www-nutraxen-com.conf /etc/nginx/sites-available/www-nutraxen-com.conf
